ABOUT HAMMERWOLD

Based in the industrial heart of Gujarat—Surat—Hammerwold was founded to solve the most pressing issue in heavy industry: connecting the right workforce with the right projects at the right time.

We are more than a job board. We are a comprehensive marketplace designed exclusively for the complexities of industrial construction, manufacturing setups, and large-scale infrastructure projects. We bring transparency, speed, and reliability to industrial hiring and contracting.

Our Mission

To digitize and streamline the industrial contracting and labor supply chain.

Our Vision

Becoming the backbone of industrial project execution across India.

Our Services

Comprehensive solutions for every stage of industrial project resourcing.

Project Marketplace

A centralized hub where enterprises can list requirements for factory setups, expansions, and infrastructure. Contractors can discover verified leads, review blueprints, and submit competitive bids securely.

Contractor Hiring

Access our vetted directory of specialized contractors (Mechanical, Electrical, Civil). Review their past projects, safety records, and client ratings before awarding critical project milestones.

Labor Contractor Network

Need 500 workers mobilized in a week? Connect with registered Labor Contractors who can supply and manage large teams of specialized and general labor, complete with compliance tracking.

Direct Worker Hiring

For precise skill gaps, browse individual worker profiles. Find certified welders, riggers, heavy machinery operators, and electricians based on proximity and verified experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Hammerwold verify contractors?

We verify company registration, GST details, past project portfolio, and safety compliance records before assigning a "Verified" badge.

Is it free to post a project?

Yes, posting standard projects is completely free for companies. We charge a nominal success fee only when a contract is awarded through the platform.

Can I hire just one worker?

Absolutely. While we facilitate large team deployments via Labor Contractors, you can also browse the 'Workers' directory to hire specialized individuals directly.
